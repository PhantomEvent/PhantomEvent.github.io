Welcome to Phantom Event Attacks

Phantom events in blockchain systems are caused by minor manipulations, which turn regular contract events into covert channels for unauthorized actions. Exploiting the innate trust people have in blockchain transactions, these events evade standard security measures to conduct unauthorized activities. They involve crafty tactics of imitating or changing smart contract events to deceive observers, front-end interfaces, and data analysis tools in the network. This misuse compromises data integrity and can result in significant asset and reputation losses..
